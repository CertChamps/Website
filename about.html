About Us

Behind the Crown

We're engineers. Of course we know the struggle of having to study even though you really don't want to. Of course we know that feeling when you have a very specific question and don't know who exactly to ask. We created CertChamps to foster an engaging and competitive environment that makes exam preparation addictive, and to provide a sense of community for you Leaving Cert students. After all, you are all in this together to reach a common goal.

We are proud to have created a platform, that reduces 'study' from your routine while still getting the work done!

Our Goals

Our main goal is to create the best possible platform for practicing maths questions. Another huge priority for us is making all content and features completely free and easily accessible to all students. We were tired of all the existing platforms asking for huge sums of money and leaving passion out the door. But we are changing that. The reason we built this was out of passion not monetisation and we are more that excited to share our work with you.
